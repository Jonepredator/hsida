My Different Black & Grey Styles

Realism tattoo

Realism or photorealism is a technique that results in artwork that looks like a photograph or a real life 3D object. ... Realistic tattoos are inspired by the realism art movement which spawned in France in the 1850s. It was a response to the dramatic and emotional styles like romanticism that preceded it.

Fine Line tattoo

Fine line tattoos can be of any size, but a popular trend at the moment is micro tattoos, the smaller lines allowing more detail in smaller areas.

Illustrative tattoo

Illustrative tattoo is a pretty broad spectrum of styling because it basically just has to look like you could find the same thing, but on a piece of paper or a canvas. Usually this means it looks like a drawing, or even a sketch...a hint of pencil on paper.

Dotwork tattoo

Dotwork tattoos are images composed entirely of single dots. This process can take a long time to complete. They have been around for thousands of years. Today, dotwork has reemerged as a trend due to the way the artwork looks when completed. When closely examined, this type of tattooing also allows you to see each individual dot. This gives the artwork more character.
