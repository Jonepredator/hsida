Who Am I

About Me

Let’s start off with where I am from. Värnamo, a small town in the southern woods of Sweden in the middle of a region named Småland. I was not born there but I was raised there. I was born 1,5 hours south of Värnamo in a town called Alvesta. I tattooed a lot of my friends before I actually started tattooing for real. And the tattoos were not always good or let’s say well made. But apparently good enough for a tattoo artist to encourage me to start drawing again and maybe become his apprentice.

Why Tattooing

Tattooing was a transition between the art you can put away when you dont want to see it and the art that is more or less permanent. I got my first tattoo when i was really young. It's between my shoulders and has faded away a lot by now. I did it in a garage at this guys home. He was the one who told me i should get in to tattooing. He thought so because he saw my drawings i brought to his place. I was indecisive of which one i should do first. Today i have them all on my body. I started drawing again but not tattooing until my wife (girlfriend at the time) bought me a tattoo kit. That was the start of my tattoo career. I still do it because of the feeling i get when i tattoo. It's special. From thousands of years in different cultures to an underground movement. Mystic and beautiful. Even if it's all visible many times, the "ritual" is special.
